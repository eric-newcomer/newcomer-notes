About Me

If you're on this website, chances are that you know me. For the people that don't, welcome! Below you will find a short bio in which I attempt to describe who I am.

To whomever it may concern:

Hi! My name is Eric Newcomer, and I live in Bellingham, Washington. I recently graduated from Sehome High School (as you can see from the picture above), where I had the privilege of being an ASB President, a Drum Major, and a student. Prior to life in Bellingham, I lived in Placentia, California (it’s near Anaheim) for ten years, before moving to the Pacific Northwest at age thirteen.

Just like any other teenage kid, I’ve had many unique experiences throughout my life that have changed the way I think and/or shaped who I am as a person. Now that a large chapter of my life has just ended and a new chapter is about to begin, I wanted to take the opportunity to reflect on some of these experiences, and share what lessons I've learned from them.

I am by no means saying that I have it all figured out, or know what I’m doing for that matter. This blog is meant for entertainment purposes, so take everything I say with a grain of salt. I used to journal a lot when I was younger, so in a way, I’m doing this to continue that hobby (and learn HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Javascript, etc along the way…)

-EN
